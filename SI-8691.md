{code}
scala> Seq(1,2) ++: Seq(3,4).view

  at scala.collection.TraversableView$NoBuilder.result(TraversableView.scala:27)
{code}

but single elements work:

{code}
scala> 2 +: Seq(3,4).view
res3: scala.collection.SeqView[Int,Seq[_]] = SeqViewA(...)
{code}
